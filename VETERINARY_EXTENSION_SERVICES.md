### C. Veterinary Service Workflow

#### **Step 1: Initial Consultation**
1. Farmer initiates contact (in-person or telemedicine)
2. Veterinarian assesses farm situation
3. Preliminary diagnosis provided
4. Samples collected if needed

#### **Step 2: Diagnosis & Prescription**
1. Diagnostic tests conducted (if required)
2. Veterinarian issues prescription
3. Treatment plan documented
4. Follow-up schedule established

#### **Step 3: Treatment & Monitoring**
1. Farmer administers treatment
2. Farm worker logs administration
3. Veterinarian monitors progress
4. Adjustments made as needed

#### **Step 4: Follow-up & Documentation**
1. Follow-up consultation scheduled
2. Results documented in FarmKonnect
3. Compliance verified
4. Records archived for audit
