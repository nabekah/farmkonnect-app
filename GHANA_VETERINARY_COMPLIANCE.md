## Executive Summary

This document outlines FarmKonnect's comprehensive compliance framework for veterinary services in Ghana, aligned with regulations from the Ghana Veterinary Council, Ministry of Food & Agriculture (MoFA), and Ghana FDA.

---

## Regulatory Bodies & Requirements

### 1. Ghana Veterinary Council (GVC)

**Authority:** Regulates veterinary practice and licensing  
**Key Requirements:**

- All veterinarians must hold valid GVC license
- Annual license renewal required
- Continuing Professional Development (CPD) mandatory
- Ethical conduct standards compliance
- Record-keeping for minimum 5 years

**FarmKonnect Implementation:**
- Veterinarian registration with license verification
- Automated license expiry alerts
- CPD tracking dashboard
- Audit trail for all veterinary activities

### 2. Ministry of Food & Agriculture (MoFA)

**Authority:** Oversees agricultural practices and animal health  
**Key Requirements:**

- Prescription records for controlled substances
- Farm health status reporting
- Livestock disease notification
- Crop health documentation
- Fish farming health protocols

**FarmKonnect Implementation:**
- Prescription audit logs with timestamps
- Disease reporting module
- Health status tracking by farming type
- Automated compliance reports

### 3. Ghana FDA (Food and Drugs Authority)

**Authority:** Regulates veterinary pharmaceuticals  
**Key Requirements:**

- Only FDA-approved medications for animals
- Controlled substance tracking
- Adverse event reporting
- Expiry date monitoring
- Pharmacy license verification

**FarmKonnect Implementation:**
- Drug approval database integration
- Controlled substance logging
- Adverse reaction tracking
- Pharmacy validation system

## Telemedicine Compliance

### Ghana Telemedicine Standards

**Requirements:**
- Licensed veterinarian conducting consultation
- Secure video communication
- Prescription issuance allowed with follow-up
- Record retention
- Patient consent documentation

**FarmKonnect Implementation:**
- Veterinarian verification before consultation
- Encrypted video conferencing
- Digital prescription issuance
- Automated consent forms
- Consultation recording (with consent)

### Telemedicine Limitations

**Restrictions:**
- Cannot replace in-person examination for complex cases
- Controlled substances require in-person verification
- Emergency cases must have backup in-person option
- Follow-up within 7 days for complex conditions

**FarmKonnect Implementation:**
- Case complexity assessment
- Automatic referral to in-person consultation
- Emergency escalation procedures
- Follow-up scheduling
